Q: Why doesn't your blog have comments?

It's easier to maintain a static site. All I have to do is upload the files to GitHub Pages and they're magically available to the world. Some day, I might set up a server to host comments.

Q: What's the story with the icon?

I needed an icon and drew something vaguely inspired by the Game of Life. Here it is in action.

Q: Which blogs do you like?

Two-Bit History. It gets into the gritty technical details of computer history. It's terribly upsetting when writers of popular history make vague allusions to technical beans rather than spilling them. Probably because they don't understand the beans. I'm looking at you, Steven "HTML is a programming language" Levy.

I'm also a fan of Julia Evans. She covers topics like networking and kernel programming in a fun and accessible way.
